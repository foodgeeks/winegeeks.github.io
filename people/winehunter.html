winehunter reviewed Tintara Cabernet Sauvignon McLaren Vale 2002 on February 12, 2005

winehunter reviewed Casa Larga Cabernet Sauvignon-Merlot New York NV on February 10, 2005

Yum. Big ripe, bright, juicy berry flavors. Very soft with light tannins which are well integrated with the fruit, showing a surprising complexity as it opened up during a couple hours of sipping. Just had cheese and crackers, but this would be a great wine with smoked meats or barbeque. Delicious.

winehunter reviewed St. Francis Old Vines Zinfandel Sonoma County 2001 on February 6, 2005

A delicious Zin. Typical for St. Francis, who I think has been making really nice Zins for several years. Rich is the key word. Spicey, jammy, and quite complex. Still on its way up the hill, I think it will improve for a few years. See the 1996. I think Winegeeks underrated this one, it&#039;s a 7.0 minimum.

winehunter reviewed St. Francis Old Vines Zinfandel Sonoma County 1996 on February 6, 2005

I had this wine at a dinner with the 01&#039; vintage Old Vines St. Francis Zin, the 94 Topolos Piner Heights Zin, and the 95 Topolos Pagani Ranch Zin. This one was my favorite. Still at the top of the hill, it was rich, complex, and spicey, with very well integrated tannin and fruit. This will still be good for a while and is probably a good indication of where the 2001 is heading.

winehunter reviewed Topolos Zinfandel Sonoma County Pagani Ranch 1995 on February 6, 2005

O.K. I actually drank this wine with baroloman at a dinner. It was drinking very nicely. Still had some tannnins left, good color and depth for a ten year old Zin. But I don&#039;t care for the distinctive minty, eucalyptus flavors. They were a bit too much for me, but fans of Heitz Martha&#039;s Cab, and some of the Joseph Phelps cabs would like this wine.

winehunter reviewed Topolos Zinfandel Sonoma County Piner Heights 1994 on February 6, 2005
