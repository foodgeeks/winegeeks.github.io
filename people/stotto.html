stotto reviewed Macari Vineyards Early Wine Chardonnay 2006 on January 23, 2008

stotto reviewed Bartenura Moscato 2006 on December 30, 2007

Excellent substitute for Champagne. Beautiful light taste that will work well as an apertif or dessert wine. Not great for dinner, but easy to drink with a light lunch or cheese.

stotto reviewed Pindar Vineyards Winter White on December 30, 2007

stotto reviewed Pindar Vineyards Sweet Scarlett on December 30, 2007

This is my "go-to" wine. Delicious with a variety of food types; especially barbeque and Italian red sauce. I prefer to drink this cold or chilled, but it is good no matter how it is served.

stotto reviewed Pindar Vineyards Summer Blush on December 30, 2007
