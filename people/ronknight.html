ronknight reviewed Keg Trading Company introduces Betty Boop! Sparkling California WinesWeibel Vineyards NV on October 13, 2011

BETTY BOOP!TM It's the Beverage that puts the "Boop" in the "Boop Oop a DoopTM"!

SPARKLING FLAVORED CHAMPAGNE STYLE ALCOHOLIC TASTING NOTES

"Betty Boop" TM Sparkling Champagne style has a beautiful, soft pink hue that is accentuated by tiny bubbles. Fresh aromas of ripe raspberries mingle with notes of sweet red cherries and white peaches. Pleasantly fruity, with a nice, naturally tangy finish, this festive sparkling wine pairs well with white chocolate, Ambrosia salad, soft white cheeses, and rich cakes. Sip ice cold in a tall fluted glass or use it as a mixer in any of your favorite cocktails.

Composed entirely of French Colombard, an offspring of the Chenin Blanc grape, Betty BoopTM Sparkling California Wines are produced in the Méthod Charmat, in which a cold secondary fermentation occurs in stainless tanks and then bottled under pressure. - Festive for all occasions – it's the the perfect 20 something date drink,that also stands up to vintage demographics who relive the Minnie the Moocher era - Alc. By Vol.: 11%; Residual Sugar: 2.8 g/L, Total Acidity: 0.6 g/100 ml

Available in limited Edition Collectible with Vintage Art Labels

"Betty Boop": known as the first and one of the most famous sex symbols on the animated screen; a symbol of America's first Great Depression era, a reminder of the more carefree days of Jazz Age flappers. Her popularity drawn largely from adult audiences, while the cartoons, while seemingly surrealistic, contained many risque/psychological elements, particularly in the "Talkartoon", Minnie the Moocher, featuring Cab Calloway and his orchestra.

The new Alcoholic Champagne style (at 11% alcohol) reflects the classic product out of France, where Betty Boop has been seen as a champagne. In England, a successful similar 'girl's beverage product' known as "BabyCham", historically successful is now marketed again as a Female 20 something demo "date drink" with a gossip fan club niche.

Betty Boop TM, the drink that puts the "Boop" in the Boop Oop a Doop! is simply Boop-alicious! and delivers a high recognition brand that buttons up this niche in select markets.
The product is exclusively licensed by King Features/Hearst Entertainment to Keg Trading Company, while bottled by Weibel Family Vineyards of Lodi, California.

The Sparkling Wines for Restaurant, On Premise Tavern, Pub & Nightclub Sales, Theme Entertainment Restaurants, Resorts and Specialty Wine Retailers is available in Brut, Raspberry and Peach Flavored Sparkling Grape Wines.
