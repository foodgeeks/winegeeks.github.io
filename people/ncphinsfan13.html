ncphinsfan13 reviewed Ricossa Barbera d'Asti 2004 on March 4, 2008

Great wine for a great price! Good berry flavors with easy going tannins with lots of liveliness in a red wine. Full-bodied but not overbearing like the zins I have been consuming lately.

ncphinsfan13 reviewed Lucien Albrecht Vieilles Vignes Gewurtraminer 2005 on January 21, 2008

Beautiful wine! Went very well with pork roast and butternut squash. Ripe pear, apple and mild honey notes with just enough spice on the end to round it out.

ncphinsfan13 reviewed La Gerla Brunello di Montalcino 2001 on January 14, 2008

Drank this before an Artesa Reserve Cabernet 1997 on my 30th birthday. A little tight when we opened it but after 1/2 hour in the glass the cherry, vanilla, and soft tannins came out. Best to decant this one for a while and let it evolve in the glass.
