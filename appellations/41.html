Sonoma County

Not only is Sonoma County larger than Napa County, but contrary to popular belief it produces more grapes as well. Over 200 wineries currently call Sonoma County home. Every important grape is grown in Sonoma County, which is a landscape filled with diverse climates and terroir.

AVAs found within Sonoma County include: Alexander Valley, Bennett Valley, Chalk Hill, Dry Creek Valley, Green Valley of Sonoma County, Knights Valley, Los Carneros, North Coast, Northern Sonoma, Rockpile, Russian River Valley, Sonoma Coast, Sonoma Mountain and Sonoma Valley.
